News / A NEW TREND FOR FASHION

In times where speed is a key factor to implement new trends into the market as fast as possible, it is important to find new possibilities to speed up the development process from a design idea to a produced garment. Especially approval procedures slow down the development process, when samples and swatches are being send around the world to get a buyer’s confirmation for a color, material or trim.

Due to Covid 19, the current world situation makes it more difficult or even impossible to travel and visit suppliers, customers or fairs. Deliveries take longer than usual and make in house dates unpredictable. The whole supply chain is affected and finding new solutions to reduce the amount of traveling or shipments are essential.
