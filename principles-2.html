Principles

“Sustainable fashion” is a buzzword that everyone likes to use these days. However, sustainability doesn’t happen overnight and for the industry, there’s still a long way to go.

We prefer to use another word instead: Responsibility.

We take our responsibility to contribute to the improvement of environmental and social performances of our entire supply chain. We insist that our processes are as environmentally friendly as possible, and partner only with  responsible manufacturers and suppliers to avoid any unethical practice.

Garment sourcing with respect for the PLANET / PEOPLE / CLIENT

GMS is a member of Business Social Compliance Initiative (BSCI) - a broad based business-driven movement that aims to monitor and assess workplace standards across the global supply chain.

Trust creates an architecture for long-lasting partnerships. Transparency is critical to accountability.

Traceability gives us data, data gives us knowledge.

So we can make fully informed decisions with our clients.

We are aware of the increasing pressure on fashion brands and retailers to document their supply chains from raw material source to importer. As early adapters of traceability tools, we provide our clients with more information on their products - a fully transparent supply chain for organic and ethical products.

GMS works only with BSCI certified suppliers and provides a platform for both suppliers and its clients which leads to long term partnerships.

Quality is the foundation of our business and we never compromise on this front. We work with a highly experienced in-house quality control team who, implementing advanced procedures, perform a detailed inspection of garments to ensure they meet and exceed industry quality standards. 

We strongly believe that digital transformation is the industrial revolution of our era.As we are optimizing old-fashioned production cycles and the traditional supply chain process by moving it from offline to online. The shift to digital enables us to provide faster and more accurate status updates to our clients, while reducing resource waste, overall costs and lead times.
