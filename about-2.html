GMS is a global garment sourcing, design and development practice trusted since 2011. Headquartered in Dubai with hubs in Hong Kong, Bangladesh and Turkey, we work with a network of well-tested manufacturing partners across the World.

For a list of established global brands and retailers, our international team of experts effectively manage the supply chain to ensure fast, flexible and cost-effective fashion services.
