Team
A GROUP OF FASHION INDUSTRY PROFESSIONALS WITH A WEALTH OF EXPERIENCE, EXPERTISE AND PROGRESSIVE IDEAS.
Rooted in collaboration and a deep understanding of the client’s requirements, our work is powered by years of experience and the shared expertise of our team members across all areas of the industry, as well as our continuous commitment to innovative ideas and technologies.
Marc ZÖLLER
Managing Director

Marc set his foot in the fashion industry as a sales person in a skater shop when he was 16 and has never looked back since.

Having studied Fashion Design and Textile Products, he worked in London as a designer, before moving to Berlin and Vienna to work as a buyer for six years.

The spectrum of his professional experience in the industry has earned Marc a thorough understanding of the marketplace. He brings a great understanding of consumer trends and applies all the lessons learnt from his extensive experience into sourcing and production.

Since joining GMS as Head of Design, Marc has been very instrumental in the company’s digital transformation, was responsible for the GMS Hong Kong office as Country Manager and is currently the GMS Managing Director based in the Dubai Headquarter

Outside of work, Marc is enthusiastic about improving his basketball skills.

Sammi WONG
Hong Kong
Country Manager, General Operations

A target-oriented team leader with creative and analytical skills and 20+ years of experience in the apparel industry with a special focus on Knit and Sweater products.

Sammi always applies her product knowledge and experience to enhance the maximum value to the company.

Working for GMS for 10 years starting as the Product Development Manager, Sammi demonstrated her ability in collaboration with the customers, suppliers and teammates. Her positive approach and mindset successfully lead the team to deal with different difficulties and she always showed her passion on fashion.

Sammi is now the Country Manager in GMS Hong Kong Office.

Mohammad KABIR
Dhaka
Country Manager, General Operations

A strategic thinker with a high level of commercial acumen and collaboration skills to manage business operations at both micro and macro levels. Seasoned in analyzing industry insights, and market trends; tenacious in ensuring customer service and sustainability.

Kabir has extensive leadership in business development, sourcing, and merchandising coupled with strong cross-functional people management expertise. Also well experienced in R&D, product development, costing, sales, planning, production, quality management, logistics, and building valuable business relationships.

He is a people-oriented person with the result-focused ability to coordinate the efforts of many to meet organizational goals with effective leadership and team building. Academic qualifications in International Business, Apparel Manufacturing & Technology, and a passion for product catered him as a professional in the fashion supply chain.
